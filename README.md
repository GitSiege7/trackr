# Trackr
> "Life is an hours game" - Sun Tzu, I think

A time tracking application for those who are motivated by progress.

## Motivation

They say it takes 10,000 hours to master something, and while that rule is definitely not hard and fast, it has some truth to it. Namely that mastery comes foremost through ***time***, and time is not a great motivator.

I've been learning Japanese for years, and the longer I learn the more I see how much longer I have ahead of me. In the day-to-day, it often feels like I make no progress at all, because progress rarely shows in the short-term. Progress is like the sun descending on the horizon, it's movements are imperceivable in the minute by minute, but in the long-term you can see the night versus the day.

I've learned, over my years of learning, that any time spent learning is progress, and when you can't watch progress, like you can't watch the sun descend (and not just because it will burn your eyes out), you can watch time.

TL;DR: If you like watching numbers go up, Trackr lets you watch numbers go up.
